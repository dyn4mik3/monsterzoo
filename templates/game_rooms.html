Quick Start Guide

Login

  • Click on the "Start a Game" link above
  • Enter in a user name in the chat box
  • Once 2 players have entered in their user name, the game will start

Game Play

  • Play cards from your hand
  • Acquire cards from The Wild using Food resources
  • Move Monster cards into your Zoo for points
  • First player to 20 wins
